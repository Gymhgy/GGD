Who we are

Sea Cadets is a US Navy-sponsored program that train its cadets in a variety of military discipline. At Grey Ghost, we drill monthly on the USS Hornet Museum in Alameda, CA.

OUR mission

The mission of the Sea Cadets is to build leaders of character by imbuing in our cadets the highest ideals of honor, respect, commitment, and service.

Grey Ghost

Grey Ghost Division is our senior program for cadets 14-17. It exposes cadets to the full rigors of a military training program.

T.S. Kearsarge

Training Ship Kearsarge is our junior program for cadets 10-13. It introduces cadets to naval life through basic instruction.

Who can join

All prospective cadets must be U.S. citizens or permanent legal residents, and meet our minimum age requirement (ages 10 through the completion of high school).
